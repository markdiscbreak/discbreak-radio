    a long time ago in manchester...

    disc break started as a monthly live music & dj night in the basement of font bar, manchester, back in 2002.

    later moving to simple bar on tib street, for a 6-year friday night residency.

    now, disc break radio has been reanimated as an online radio station. a platform for djs, presenters, selectors, and music lovers.

    the station is dance music orientated, covering house to hip-hop, soul, jazz and funk from all corners of the globe.

    based in stockport, manchester, but boasting an international lineup of djs, disc break is a 24/7 online radio station.

    no ads. just good music.

    disc break hosts playlists, guest dj mixes, and label showcases from some of the best underground and undiscovered selectors.

    original disc break flyer, 2002 · the font bar, manchester
