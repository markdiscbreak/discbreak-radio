Disc Break is an independent online radio station broadcasting from Manchester. No ads, no algorithms — just handpicked music from DJs who genuinely love what they play.

We started as a community project to give established and undiscovered DJs a platform. Every show is live, every mix is curated, and every track is chosen with care.

Broadcasting 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Tune in from anywhere in the world.
